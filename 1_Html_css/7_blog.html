Cats

2019-06-20

Say hello to our dormitory cat, Piggy! You can easily guess why he's called that. He's pretty fat, and very soft to touch...

He likes being petted, basking in the sunlight, and eating some snacks. His favorite food is Ciao Churu: a soup-like catfood that tastes like tuna, salmon, or chicken. But he's so generous that he sometimes yields his Churu to a younger cat.

Look! he's taking a nap in the bush.
